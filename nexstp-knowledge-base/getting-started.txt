=============================================
WELCOME TO NEXSTP
=============================================

Welcome to Nexstp - your premier multi-tenant marketplace for digital products and creative works! Whether you're a buyer looking for amazing products or a creator ready to start selling, this guide will help you get started in just 15 minutes.

What is Nexstp?
- Multi-tenant marketplace with individual store fronts
- Buy and sell digital products, courses, templates, and more
- Exclusive Launchpad deals with time-limited discounts
- Real-time chat between buyers and sellers
- Secure payments via Stripe
- Lifetime access to purchased products

=============================================
FOR SELLERS: GETTING STARTED
=============================================

STEP 1: SIGN UP & VERIFY
------------------------

Start your selling journey:

1. Create Nexstp account (see Buyer Step 1)
2. Log into your account
3. Go to Dashboard
4. Click "Start Selling"
5. Complete Stripe Connect onboarding:
   - Business type (individual/company)
   - Personal information
   - Business details (if applicable)
   - Bank account for payouts
   - Identity verification (government ID)
6. Wait for Stripe approval (usually 1-2 days)
7. Receive confirmation email
8. Ready to sell!

Why Stripe Connect?
- Secure payment processing
- Automatic payouts
- Handle taxes and compliance
- Support multiple currencies
- Industry standard for marketplaces

STEP 2: SET UP YOUR STORE
-------------------------

Create your brand:

1. Go to Dashboard > Store Settings
2. Store Information:
   - Store name
   - Store slug (URL: nexstp.com/store/your-slug)
   - Description (tell your story!)
   - Store logo (recommended: 400x400px)
   - Banner image (optional: 1200x400px)
   - Contact email
3. Store Policies:
   - Default refund policy
   - Shipping info (if applicable)
   - Terms and conditions
4. Social Links (optional):
   - Website
   - Twitter
   - Instagram
   - LinkedIn
5. Click "Save Store"

Your store URL: https://nexstp.com/store/[your-slug]
Share this link to promote your store!

STEP 3: CREATE YOUR FIRST PRODUCT
---------------------------------

List your first product:

1. Go to Dashboard > Products
2. Click "Create Product"
3. Product Details:
   - Title (clear and descriptive)
   - Description (sell the benefits!)
   - Price (USD, minimum $1)
   - Category (select appropriate)
   - Tags (help buyers find it)
4. Product Images:
   - Upload 1-5 images
   - First image is thumbnail
   - Recommended: 1200x800px
   - Max 5MB per image
5. Product Files (digital products):
   - Upload downloadable files
   - ZIP files for multiple items
   - Max file size: check current limits
6. Refund Policy:
   - No refunds
   - 7 days
   - 14 days
   - 30 days money-back guarantee
7. Click "Publish Product"

Product Tips:
- Use high-quality images
- Write compelling descriptions
- Price competitively
- Choose relevant categories
- Add helpful tags
- Offer fair refund policy

STEP 4: CREATE A LAUNCHPAD
--------------------------

Launch with special pricing:

1. Go to Dashboard > Products
2. Select an existing product
3. Click "Create Launchpad"
4. Launchpad Settings:
   - Launch price (must be less than regular price)
   - Duration in hours (24-168 hours)
   - Start date and time
   - Optional: Limited quantity
5. Preview your launchpad
6. Click "Launch"
7. Launchpad goes live at scheduled time
8. Appears on Launchpads page
9. Countdown begins!
10. Auto-ends when timer expires

Launchpad Benefits:
- Generate buzz for new products
- Early revenue from eager buyers
- Build customer base quickly
- Test pricing strategies
- Create urgency with timers

Launchpad Best Practices:
- 20-50% discount is attractive
- 48-72 hours duration works well
- Promote before launch
- Engage with buyers during launchpad
- Follow up after launch ends

STEP 5: MANAGE ORDERS
---------------------

Handle your sales:

1. Go to Dashboard > Orders
2. View all orders:
   - Order number
   - Buyer information
   - Order date
   - Amount
   - Status
   - Platform fee
3. Order details:
   - Click any order to view
   - See buyer contact info
   - View items purchased
   - Check payment status
   - Track payouts
4. Order statuses:
   - Pending: Payment processing
   - Completed: Payment successful
   - Refunded: Refund issued
   - Cancelled: Order cancelled

For digital products:
- Delivery is automatic
- Buyer gets instant access
- No shipping required
- Product added to buyer's Library

For physical products:
- Mark as shipped
- Add tracking number
- Update order status
- Communicate with buyer

Build relationships:

STEP 7: TRACK YOUR EARNINGS
---------------------------

Monitor your success:

1. Go to Dashboard > Analytics
2. View key metrics:
   - Total revenue
   - Number of orders
   - Product views
   - Conversion rate
   - Best-selling products
   - Revenue by time period
3. Payout Information:
   - View pending payouts
   - Payout history
   - Payout schedule (2-7 days)
   - Bank account details

Understanding Platform Fees:
- Platform fee: 10% of each sale
- Stripe processing: ~2.9% + $0.30
- Example: $100 sale
  * Buyer pays: $100
  * Platform fee: $10
  * Stripe fee: ~$3.20
  * You receive: ~$86.80

Payouts:
- Automatic via Stripe Connect
- Rolling 2-7 day schedule
- Direct to your bank account
- View in Dashboard > Payouts
