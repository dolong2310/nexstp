Troubleshooting Guide - Nexstp Marketplace

Common Issues and Solutions

=============================================
LOGIN AND AUTHENTICATION ISSUES
=============================================

PROBLEM: Cannot log in - "Invalid credentials" error
SOLUTIONS:
1. Verify your email and password are correct
2. Check that Caps Lock is OFF
3. Ensure you're using the email you registered with
4. Try copying and pasting password from password manager
5. Clear browser cookies for nexstp.com
6. Try incognito/private browsing mode
7. Reset your password using "Forgot your password?" link
8. Check if email is verified (check inbox for verification email)

PROBLEM: "Please verify your email before signing in"
SOLUTIONS:
1. Check your email inbox for verification link
2. Check spam/junk folder
3. Search for emails from noreply@nexstp.com
4. Click "Resend Verification Email" on sign-in page
5. Wait a few minutes for email to arrive
6. Try a different email if issue persists
7. Contact support: support@nexstp.com

PROBLEM: Password reset email not received
SOLUTIONS:
1. Wait 5-10 minutes (may be delayed)
2. Check spam/junk/promotions folders
3. Search inbox for "Nexstp" or "Password Reset"
4. Add noreply@nexstp.com to contacts
5. Try resending reset email
6. Check email address is correct
7. Ask IT to whitelist our domain (work emails)
8. Try different email address
9. Contact support if still not received

PROBLEM: Reset link says "Invalid or expired token"
SOLUTIONS:
1. Reset links expire after 24 hours - request new one
2. Use the most recent reset email
3. Copy full URL from email (may be cut off)
4. Don't click link multiple times
5. Request fresh reset link
6. Complete reset within 24 hours

PROBLEM: "Session expired" keeps appearing
SOLUTIONS:
1. Enable cookies in browser settings
2. Clear browser cache and cookies
3. Disable aggressive privacy settings
4. Add nexstp.com to browser exceptions
5. Try different browser
6. Disable VPN temporarily
7. Check browser extensions aren't blocking cookies
8. Update browser to latest version

PROBLEM: Cannot sign up - "Username already taken"
SOLUTIONS:
1. Choose a different username
2. Try adding numbers or underscores
3. Username must be unique across platform
4. Check if you already have an account
5. Try "Sign In" if you forgot you registered

PROBLEM: Logged out unexpectedly
SOLUTIONS:
1. Session timeout after period of inactivity
2. Browser cleared cookies
3. Someone else logged into your account (change password!)
4. Server maintenance (temporary)
5. Check https://status.nexstp.com for platform status

=============================================
PAYMENT & CHECKOUT ISSUES
=============================================

PROBLEM: Checkout button not working
SOLUTIONS:
1. Ensure you're signed in
2. Check cart has items
3. Disable ad blockers for nexstp.com
4. Enable pop-ups for Stripe checkout
5. Clear browser cache
6. Try different browser (Chrome recommended)
7. Check internet connection
8. Wait for page to fully load
9. Hard refresh page (Ctrl/Cmd + Shift + R)

PROBLEM: Payment declined or failed
SOLUTIONS:
1. Verify card details are correct
2. Check card has sufficient funds
3. Check card is not expired
4. Contact your bank (may be blocking transaction)
5. Try different payment method
6. Check card supports international transactions (we use Stripe)
7. Disable VPN (some cards block VPN transactions)
8. Try a different card
9. Use alternative payment (Apple Pay, Google Pay)

PROBLEM: Payment succeeded but product not in Library
SOLUTIONS:
1. Wait 1-2 minutes for processing
2. Hard refresh Library page
3. Check your email for order confirmation
4. Log out and log back in
5. Check Orders page to verify purchase
6. Clear browser cache
7. Contact support with order number
8. Check spam folder for confirmation email

PROBLEM: Charged multiple times
SOLUTIONS:
1. Check if these are pending authorizations (will drop off)
2. Don't click "Pay" button multiple times
3. Contact support immediately: support@nexstp.com
4. Include: Order numbers, amount charged, payment method
5. We'll investigate and refund duplicates within 24-48 hours
6. Check with your bank about pending vs. completed charges

PROBLEM: Cannot add to cart
SOLUTIONS:
1. Make sure you're signed in
2. Check product is still available
3. Launchpad may have expired
4. Clear browser cache and cookies
5. Try different browser
6. Disable browser extensions
7. Check JavaScript is enabled
8. Hard refresh the page

PROBLEM: "Invalid products in cart" error
SOLUTIONS:
1. Product may have been removed by seller
2. Launchpad may have expired
3. Price may have changed
4. Clear cart and re-add products
5. Refresh product page to see current status
6. Contact seller if product disappeared
7. Check Library - you may already own it

PROBLEM: Stripe checkout page won't load
SOLUTIONS:
1. Allow pop-ups for nexstp.com
2. Disable ad blockers
3. Disable browser extensions
4. Check firewall settings
5. Try different browser
6. Check internet connection
7. Ensure Stripe.com is not blocked
8. Try incognito mode
9. Contact support if consistently failing

=============================================
SELLER & STRIPE ISSUES
=============================================

PROBLEM: Cannot start selling - Stripe not connected
SOLUTIONS:
1. Complete Stripe Connect onboarding
2. Go to Dashboard > Settings > Stripe
3. Click "Connect Stripe Account"
4. Complete all required information
5. Verify identity with government ID
6. Add bank account details
7. Wait for Stripe approval (1-2 days usually)
8. Check email for Stripe verification requests
9. Contact Stripe support if rejected

PROBLEM: Stripe verification pending or rejected
SOLUTIONS:
1. Check email from Stripe for additional requirements
2. Ensure all information is accurate
3. Provide clear, unedited ID photos
4. Business name must match legal documents
5. Bank account must be in your name
6. Some countries have additional requirements
7. Contact Stripe support: https://support.stripe.com
8. Re-submit verification with correct info
9. Cannot sell until verified

PROBLEM: Not receiving payouts
SOLUTIONS:
1. Check Stripe dashboard for payout status
2. Verify bank account is correct
3. Payouts take 2-7 business days
4. Check for negative balance
5. Verify your account is not on hold
6. Check if payout method is valid
7. Contact Stripe for payout issues
8. Ensure no disputes or chargebacks pending
9. Verify you met minimum payout amount (if any)

PROBLEM: Payout amount seems wrong
SOLUTIONS:
1. Remember 10% platform fee is deducted
2. Stripe processing fees (~2.9% + $0.30) also deducted
3. Currency conversion may apply
4. Check Dashboard > Earnings for breakdown
5. Refunds reduce payout amount
6. Disputed transactions may be held
7. View detailed transaction history
8. Contact support for payout calculations

PROBLEM: Cannot create product
SOLUTIONS:
1. Ensure Stripe account is verified and approved
2. Check all required fields are filled
3. Upload at least one product image
4. Price must be minimum $1
5. Product title required (max length check)
6. Category must be selected
7. Description must be filled
8. Check file size limits for images
9. Try saving draft first
10. Clear browser cache and retry

PROBLEM: Product images not uploading
SOLUTIONS:
1. Check file size (max 5MB per image)
2. Use supported formats: JPG, PNG, GIF, WEBP
3. Ensure stable internet connection
4. Try uploading one image at a time
5. Compress images if too large
6. Clear browser cache
7. Try different browser
8. Disable browser extensions
9. Check firewall/antivirus settings
10. Try incognito mode

4. Check available disk space

LAUNCHPAD ISSUES
=============================================

PROBLEM: Cannot purchase Launchpad
SOLUTIONS:
1. Check if launchpad has ended (timer at 0)
2. Verify you're signed in
3. Check if you already purchased it
4. Launchpad may be sold out (if limited quantity)

SOLUTIONS:
1. Check your device time/timezone is correct
2. Timer is in your local timezone
3. Hard refresh page for accurate time
4. Count down continues until exactly 0
5. Server time is authoritative

2. Launchpad may have ended exactly when you purchased
3. Price shown at checkout is price you pay
4. Check order confirmation email

PROBLEM: Cannot create Launchpad (seller)
SOLUTIONS:
1. Must have existing product to create launchpad
2. Launch price must be less than regular price
3. Duration must be at least 24 hours
4. Start date cannot be in the past
5. Check all required fields
6. Ensure product has all required info
7. Try creating from product page
8. Clear cache and retry

REFUND ISSUES
=============================================

PROBLEM: Cannot request refund
SOLUTIONS:
1. Check seller's refund policy
2. Ensure within refund window (7, 14, or 30 days)
3. Go to Orders > Order Details > Request Refund
4. Must be signed in
5. Provide reason for refund
6. Can only request once per order
7. "No refunds" products cannot be refunded
8. Contact seller first

PROBLEM: Refund request denied
SOLUTIONS:
1. Review seller's refund policy
2. You may be outside refund window
3. Digital products downloaded may not be eligible
4. Contact seller to discuss
5. Escalate to support if policy not honored
6. Provide evidence if product was not as described
7. May dispute via payment method

PROBLEM: Refund not received
SOLUTIONS:
1. Refunds take 5-10 business days
2. Refunded to original payment method
3. Check with your bank/card issuer
4. Verify refund was approved (check email)
5. Contact support with refund ID
6. May take longer for international cards
7. Check order status shows "Refunded"

CLEAR CACHE (ALL BROWSERS)
-------------------------

Chrome:
- Ctrl/Cmd + Shift + Delete
- Select "All time"
- Check "Cookies" and "Cached images"
- Click "Clear data"

Firefox:
- Ctrl/Cmd + Shift + Delete
- Select "Everything"
- Check all boxes
- Click "Clear Now"

Safari:
- Safari > Clear History
- Select "All history"
- Click "Clear History"

Edge:
- Ctrl + Shift + Delete
- Select "All time"
- Check all boxes
- Click "Clear now"
