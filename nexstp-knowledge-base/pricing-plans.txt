FEE STRUCTURE DETAILS
=============================================

PLATFORM FEE: 10%
-----------------

What it covers:
- Marketplace infrastructure
- Hosting and servers
- Payment processing integration
- Customer support
- Platform development
- Marketing and promotion
- Fraud protection
- Security and compliance
- Bandwidth and storage
- Platform maintenance

When it's charged:
- Automatically deducted via Stripe Connect
- Only on completed sales
- No charge for refunds
- No charge for failed payments

STRIPE PROCESSING FEES
----------------------

Standard Rates (US):
- 2.9% + $0.30 per successful card charge
- Slightly higher for international cards
- Additional fees for currency conversion
- Lower rates for high volume (contact Stripe)

What Stripe handles:
- Payment processing
- Card network fees
- Fraud prevention
- PCI compliance
- Chargebacks protection
- Payout management
- Tax collection (where applicable)

REFUNDS & FEES
--------------

If you issue a refund:
- Platform fee is refunded to you
- Stripe processing fee is NOT refunded
- You bear the Stripe fee cost

Example:
$100 sale → You received $86.80
Refund issued:
- Buyer receives full $100 back
- You pay back $90 (product price - platform fee)
- You lose ~$3.20 (Stripe processing fee)

Partial refunds:
- Fees calculated proportionally
- Platform fee refunded proportionally
- Original Stripe fee not refunded
